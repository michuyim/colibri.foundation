The Colibri Foundation

Welcome to The Colibri Foundation

The Colibri Foundation, since its inception in 2005, has been a beacon of hope and creativity. Born from a shared vision to harness the transformative power of art, our journey reflects a commitment to mental health and community well-being.

We’re building a world where everyone has the power to shape their lives. Here’s how to talk to someone who may be struggling with their mental health.


Suicide Prevention and Depression Education and Awareness


The U.S Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) reports a continued increase in individuals suffering from depression, making suicide one of the leading causes of death in our country. Some leaders in the mental health field believe that our deteriorating mental health care system is partially to blame.

We believe it’s important to share resources and shed light on this highly-stigmatized topic of issues surrounding depression and mental health. The Colibri Foundation is, therefore, stepping up and using art to reach out to the youth at risk of losing their lives due to poor mental health and stability. Our team comprises of leaders in the field of mental health and suicide prevention advocacy, support, and education.
